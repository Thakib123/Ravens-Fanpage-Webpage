# Ravens-Fanpage-Webpage

https://cis-linux2.temple.edu/tomcat10/sp25_3344_tuo46790/index.html#/home

A fan-made website dedicated to the Baltimore Ravens. This project organizes team information, stats, media, and interactive fan content into easy-to-navigate sections.  

---

## Tabs / Sections

### 1. Home
- Landing page introducing visitors to the fanpage.  
- Includes team logo, banner, welcome message, and the latest Ravens news or headlines.  

### 2. Team History
- Covers the history of the Baltimore Ravens since their founding in 1996.  
- Highlights Super Bowl wins (XXXV, XLVII), legendary players, and major milestones.  

### 3. Roster
- Current list of players and coaching staff.  
- Organized by offense, defense, and special teams.  
- Includes player photos, jersey numbers, and positions.  

### 4. Schedule & Results
- Full season schedule with dates, opponents, and kickoff times.  
- Displays past game results and key divisional matchups.  

### 5. Stats & Records
- Team and player statistics from current and past seasons.  
- Offensive leaders (passing, rushing, receiving) and defensive leaders (tackles, sacks, interceptions).  
- Franchise records and playoff history.  

### 6. Media Gallery
- Collection of photos and videos.  
- Game highlights, wallpapers, and behind-the-scenes content.  

### 7. Fan Zone
- Interactive space for Ravens fans.  
- Polls (e.g., “Player of the Week”), trivia, quizzes, and fan-submitted content.  

### 8. About the Fanpage
- Explains the purpose of the fanpage (fan-created, not affiliated with the official Baltimore Ravens).  
- Credits contributors and developers.
